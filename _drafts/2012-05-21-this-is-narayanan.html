This is Narayan ,
A day dreamer , that's how I see myself . I spent my time  in college like everyone else in movies , videogames and music, yes a lot of highs as well , in mundane I do some art . Sketches ,photoshop ,motion graphics ,photography ,scribbling .At this point of life I feel like I am wandering  around a like a zombie without purpose or will . Whats life worth is what you make of it ? Right ? I dont have the zest I had a couple of years ago . What are we doing in this planet ? Aren't we as significant as we thought we were . Are we just the speckles of dust amidst a storm ? Or are we the magnanimous body where  billions of cells live ? Why am I trying to find who I am ? Its the paradox I have been running over and over again . Me looking for me by me . Now there is an infinite loop which whiles away my time .
At a point I am too tired trying to find answers for the questions that arise . Reading a person's blog inspired me to post out my thoughts, insights and the stories I had been burying in my notes till now .Thank you .
