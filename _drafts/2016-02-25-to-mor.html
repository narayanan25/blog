Portrait of a friend. Mor is a great stop-motion-animator. You can find her creative porfolio here. I was lucky to meet her in San Francisco. She is has a very unique authentic indigenous style. And there is a flavor in all of her work. I decided to do this painting of her as her birthday gift.
This is by far my favorite painting, My appreciation for realism has had conflicts with my eagerness to explore the free flow of strokes and use of vibrant rendering.
